Text Adventure

Objective:

Functions:

  • characterName.getName(); --shows character's name
  • characterName.getHealth(); --shows character's health level
  • characterName.getArmor(); --shows character's amount of armor
  • characterName.getInventory(); --shows character's inventory
  • characterName.attack(enemyName); --Attacks enemy
  • characterName.useItem(itemName); --Your character will use an item that may help your attacking powers and/or health or hurt them. Use wisely!
    • Item names: mysteryItem1, mysteryItem2, mysteryItem3, mysteryItem4

Other notes:

  • If your health level drops below zero, you will fail the game, and you will have to start over.
  • Likewise, an enemy is not considered defeated until its health and armor levels drop below zero. You will receive a notification that the enemy has been defeated when this happens.
  • Your character's name is Alpha. Thus, when typing in commands, refer to your character as Alpha.
  • Press the start button to begin. You can press the start button again at any time to restart the game.
