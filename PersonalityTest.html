Welcome to the Personality Test: Late for Class webpage! Here are some notes before you begin:

  • To ensure the most accurate results, you will answer all questions regardless of what choices you make.
  • Answer all questions honestly. What would you really do in that situation?
  • That being said, assume that you are capable of all presented options. Don't avoid picking something just because you think you couldn't do it--this is about personality, not abilities.
